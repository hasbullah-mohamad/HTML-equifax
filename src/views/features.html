Get the right fit

CrediBot has a range or product options to align with your business needs. Whether you need a quick, simple solution; a standalone bespoke integration; or something in between, CrediBot has you covered.

AI Driven Payment Negotiation

CrediBot's industry-leading AI payment negotiation engine responsively calculates and optimises the best payment options for your customers. Proprietary personalised algorithms achieve results almost identical to a human consultant, and are adaptive to your specified parameters and limits.

Flexible Payment Options

When your customers can take control of their repayments, they are more likely to commit. CrediBot offers flexible payment options including instant payments, payment plans and multiple payment methods including Credit Card, Debit Card, Direct Debit and third party integrations. CrediBot empowers your customers to easily resolve their account with payment options that suit their needs.

Automated Workflows

CrediBot improves efficiency with automated workflows. Simply set your communications strategy and let CrediBot do the rest, or utilise the powerful segmentation tools in CrediBot Enterprise to automatically optimise workflows for different customer segments. Workflows can even be set before accounts become overdue to send customers reminder messages, effectively automating and optimising the entire customer lifecycle.

Instant payments

With integrated payment gateways, you're automatically set up to accept payments instantly from your customers.

Trust Account Payment Processing

CrediBot removes the hassle of separate integrations with in-house payment processing systems by offering secure Credit Card and Direct Debit processing services with daily remittance of funds.
